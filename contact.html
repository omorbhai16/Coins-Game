Why did we choose only email?

the many ways to communicate on digital platforms, we give the most importance to email. There are several logical and technical reasons behind this:

1. Written Evidence and Records: When you communicate via email, there is a permanent record of every conversation between you and us. This record helps us resolve any payment or account related issues in the future, which will save us a lot of time.

2. Information Security: Email is much more secure than messenger or other social media platforms. There is no risk of your personal information or user ID being leaked.

3. Direct Developer Support: Our emails are monitored directly by our core technical team. As a result, you are getting a direct solution from an expert instead of a general moderator. Which will be able to resolve any major issues you may have very quickly.

4. Large file sharing: Email is the most effective way to send proof of payment or screenshots or videos of any technical issues in the game. And email allows you to write everything in a very neat way.

What topics will you email us about?

You can contact us in any of the following situations during your gaming journey:

1. Payment or Withdrawal Support:
If you have not received the money in your bKash or Rocket account within a certain period of time (e.g. 24-48 hours) after withdrawing your earned coins, then please email us without delay. Be sure to provide your withdrawal time and transaction ID in the email so that we can resolve your issue.

2. Account Verification and Security:
If you are having trouble logging into your account or if you believe your account security has been compromised, please let us know immediately. We will verify your identity and help you recover your account.

3. Technical Bug or Error Report:
If you find any technical issues or 'bugs' while playing the game (such as coins not being added or the screen hanging), please let us know with a screenshot. Your report will help us improve the game and take it further in the future!

4. Advertising Issues:
Our platform displays ads from networks like Monitag and other ad companies. If any ad is causing a major disruption to your gaming experience or is offensive, please email us with the details of the ad.
