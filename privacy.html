Privacy Policy

Welcome. We give the highest priority to the privacy and protection of personal information of our users. The main purpose of this privacy policy page is to explain in detail what information we collect from you when you play our coin earning game, how we use it and how we ensure the security of your information.

Before playing our coin earning game, please read our privacy policy in full. This will give you a good idea about our privacy policy.

1. What information do we collect?

Our game is designed in such a way that we do not need any unnecessary personal information from users. However, we do collect certain information to ensure the proper functioning of the game and the payment system, making it easier for our team members to work and for users to receive proper payments:

(1) Unique Player ID (UID):

A unique identification number or UID is generated for each user upon first entering the game. This is mainly used to track the user's game progress and earned coins. We have designed it in such a way that one user's ID will not match another user's!

(2) Financial or payment information:

When you go to withdraw your earned coins as money, we collect the bKash or Rocket mobile number you provided to complete the payment, so that we can send the money to the correct place.

(3) Device and Technical Information:
We may collect some general data about your device type, operating system, and IP address to troubleshoot game errors or technical issues and to maintain the performance of the main interface.

2. How do we use the information we collect?

We use the information we collect from you with the utmost confidentiality only for game development and user experience improvement. We can 100% guarantee that this will not cause any problems in the user's personal life:

(1) Payment Processing:
Your bKash or Rocket number is only used to successfully deliver the money you requested. After the payment is successful, this information is stored in a secure archive, so you won't have any problems in real life!
(2) Level and Score Tracking:

Data is updated in real-time under your UID to ensure that you don't lose track of your earned coins and level-up information.

(3) Ad Optimization:

The Adsterra ad network used in our game does not show any 18+ or excessively bad ads, and the user will see the ad if they want to, and will not see the ad if they don't want to, no ads are forced on the user.

(1)Our site and game rely on third-party ad networks for monetization or revenue. (2) These cookies cannot access any personal files or secret passwords on your device. If you wish, you can disable or turn off cookies at any time by going to your browser settings. However, doing so may cause problems with loading some game features or ads.

4. Information security and protection:

(1) We take our users' data security seriously. Your payment number and user ID are stored encrypted on a secure server, which is only available to us.

(2) Our database undergoes regular security verification, which keeps your account safe from hacks or unauthorized access.
